Gym Management System
=====================

A complete Gym Management System for Odoo that helps manage members,
trainers, workout plans, attendance, memberships, and payment tracking efficiently.

Configuration
=============

* No additional configurations needed
* If you need SMS and WhatsApp integration, additional third-party services
  and configurations are required

Features
========

* Member Management
* Membership Plan Management
* Trainer Management
* Attendance Tracking
* Assigned Workout Plans
* Payment Reports & Analytics
* Dashboard & Calendar Views
* Membership Renewal Tracking
